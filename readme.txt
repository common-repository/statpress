== Description ==

The first real-time plugin dedicated to the management of web traffic statistics.

Once StatPress has been activated it immediately starts to collect statistics information.
Using StatPress you could spy your visitors while they are surfing your blog or check which are the preferred pages, posts and categories.
In the Dashboard menu you will find the StatPress page where you could look up the statistics (overview or detailed).

Upload Statpress directory in wp-content/plugins/ . Then just activate it on your plugin management page.
You are ready!!!
